Our Mission

The Citizen Grievance System is designed to empower individuals to report civic issues such as garbage collection, road damage, streetlight failures, and water leakage. It ensures that every voice is heard and acted upon.

Our mission is to create a transparent, responsive, and efficient ecosystem where citizens can directly connect with authorities for quick resolutions.
