Help & User Guide

A step-by-step guide to help you navigate and use the E-Grievance Management System efficiently.

🧾

Step 1: Register or Login

To access the E-Grievance system, first create an account if you are a new user. Enter your basic details such as full name, email ID, and password. If you’re already registered, simply log in using your credentials. Once logged in, you’ll be redirected to your personal dashboard where you can file and track grievances.

📨

Step 2: File a New Complaint

Click on the “File Complaint” button in your dashboard. You’ll see a form asking for complaint details — category (e.g., sanitation, electricity, etc.), location, description, and optional file uploads (photo, PDF, or video evidence). After submission, you’ll receive a unique Complaint ID for tracking.

📊

Step 3: Track Complaint Status

Navigate to the “View Complaints” section to monitor progress. Each complaint shows its current status:

  • Pending: Awaiting officer review
  • In Progress: Action initiated by officer
  • Resolved: Complaint successfully closed
You can also filter complaints by date, category, or status for easy viewing.

👮‍♂️

Step 4: Officer Review & Action

Once submitted, your complaint is automatically routed to the concerned officer based on location and category. Officers can update the complaint with remarks or mark it as “In Progress” when action begins. You’ll receive notifications or see status updates in real-time on your dashboard.

✅

Step 5: Resolution & Feedback

When the issue is resolved, the officer marks it as “Resolved.” You can review the resolution, verify the action taken, and provide feedback or rating for service quality. If not satisfied, you may reopen the complaint for further review.

☎️

Need Further Help?

If you face any difficulties using the portal, visit the Contact Us page. You can reach out through email at support@egrievance.in or call the helpline number available on the website. Our support team is available Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
